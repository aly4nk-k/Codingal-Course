Definitions of School Subjects

Mathematics
The study of numbers, shapes, patterns, and calculations. It helps people solve problems, measure things, and understand how the world works in everyday life, science, and technology.
Science
The study of the natural world through experiments, observations, and discoveries. It helps us understand how things work, including living things, space, energy, and matter.
English
The study of reading, writing, speaking, and understanding the English language. It helps people communicate clearly, express ideas, and improve their vocabulary and grammar
Computer science (Coding)
The study of computers, technology, and programming. It helps people understand how software and digital systems work to solve problems and create new technology.
Physical Education
The study of fitness, sports, and physical activities. It helps improve health, teamwork, coordination, and overall physical strength.
