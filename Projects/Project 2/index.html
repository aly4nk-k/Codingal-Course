Mini Biography: Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a famous scientist, physicist, and mathematician known for changing the way people understand space, time, and gravity.

Early Life

He was born in Ulm, Germany, and loved solving problems and exploring science from a young age.

Important Achievement

He developed the Theory of Relativity and introduced the famous equation: e=mc2. He is remembered as one of the smartest scientists in history.

One value that made him special was curiosity and imagination.

Correction Corner

He was only a scientist. He was also a teacher, writer, and thinker who spoke about peace and education.

Interesting Examples

he also worked with other scientists on atoms.( for example: H20)

Words That Inspire

He once said,
“Imagination is more important than knowledge.”

His discoveries inspired generations of scientists around the world.

My Reflection

I admire Albert Einstein because he showed that curiosity, creativity, and hard work can help people discover amazing things about the universe.
