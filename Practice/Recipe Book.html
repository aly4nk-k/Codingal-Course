Recipe#1

Fried Rice
A classic chinese dish made from rice and topped with onions, eggs.

How to Make (steps)

  1. Cook 2 cups of rice and let it cool
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan
  3. Add chopped vegetables of your choice and cook for 3-4 minutes
  4. Push the vegetables aside and scramble 1 egg in the pan
  5. Add the rice and mix everything together
  6. Pour in 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  7. Stir and cook for 2 more minutes
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

Recipe#2

Pancakes
soft, flat cakes made from batter and cooked on a pan, usually eaten with syrup, fruits, or butter.

How to Make (steps)

  1. Mix 1 cup flour, 1 egg, 1 cup milk, and 1 tablespoon sugar in a bowl
  2. Stir until the batter is smooth
  3. Heat a lightly oiled pan
  4. Pour some batter into the pan
  5. Cook until bubbles appear, then flip it
  6. Cook the other side until golden brown
  7. Serve with syrup, honey, or fruits
  8. Enjoy!

Recipe#3

Spahgetti
a popular pasta dish made with long, thin noodles usually served with sauce, meat, or cheese.

How to Make (steps)

  1. Boil water in a pot and add a little salt
  2. Add spaghetti noodles and cook for 8–10 minutes.
  3. Drain the water from the noodles
  4. Heat pasta sauce in a pan
  5. Add the spaghetti to the sauce and mix well
  6. Cook for 2 more minutes
  7. Serve hot with cheese if you like
  8. Enjoy!
